About the Artist


I can't remember a time without art. When I was old enough to hold a pencil,
I began drawing. I started by drawing my pets and my favorite animals, and
eventually grew to appreciate the creativity and originality of tattoo art.
As time passed, I stepped away from art and focused more on traditional
studies such as science and technology and would only draw as time allowed.
I am now finding balance in life that allows me to pursue my other interests as
well as exploring my artistic abilities. I understand that creating art is a gift,
and I would like to use this gift to bring joy to others.
