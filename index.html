About the Artist


I began drawing, when I was old enough to hold a pencil. I started by drawing my pets and my favorite animals, and eventually grew to appreciate the creativity and originality of tattoo art. As time passed, I stepped away from art and focused more on traditional studies such as science and technology and would only draw sporadically or as time allowed. I am now finding the balance allows me to pursue my interests as well as exploring my artistic abilities. I understand that producing art that people can enjoy is a gift, and I would like to use this gift to help bring a little joy to others.
